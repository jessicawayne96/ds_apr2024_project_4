Welcome to Our Superhero Analysis and Predictive Model

This project features two interactive dashboards: one showcasing a comprehensive dataset of over 600 superheroes, detailing their physical attributes, superpowers, and affiliations, which can be filtered by gender and alignment; and another focused on our machine learning model, which predicts battle outcomes between superheroes and villains based on character attributes and special abilities.
